Mission And Vision

Our goal is to clearly communicate the message of hope and eternal salvation found only in Jesus Chirst. This hope and eternal salvation is not found in religion or organization, but only in a living, personal and dynamic relationship with Jesus Christ. Our mission at NLCF is to know Christ and to make Him Known. Our vision is transforming lives and impacting the world for Jesus Christ by becoming a spiritually functioning, divinely dependent, and praying Christian community.
